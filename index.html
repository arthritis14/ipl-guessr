How to Play

Guess today's mystery IPL player in 8 tries.

Each guess fills a row of 8 columns: the player's name, then Team, Country, Role, Bat Hand, Age, Cap Status and Seasons. The last 7 are colour-coded against today's answer:

Green ✓ — exact match. e.g. you guess a CSK player and the answer plays for CSK too.

Yellow ≈ — close, but not exact:

  • Country: a different country, but the same continent (e.g. India and Sri Lanka are both Asia).
  • Role: a similar playing position. Yellow appears between:
    • Batter ↔ All-Rounder
    • All-Rounder ↔ Batter, Fast Bowler or Spin Bowler
    • Fast Bowler ↔ All-Rounder or Spin Bowler
    • Spin Bowler ↔ All-Rounder or Fast Bowler
  • Age or Seasons: within 3 of the answer.

Grey ✗ — no match on any of the above.

For Age and Seasons, an arrow shows which way the answer lies: ⬆ higher than your guess, or ⬇ lower.
